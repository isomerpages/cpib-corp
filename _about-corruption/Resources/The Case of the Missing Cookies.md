<p><em>The Case of the Missing Cookies</em> is a picture book for pre-schoolers,
co-created with students from St Joseph's Institution and CPIB.</p>
<p>This story tells of how Kopi Lim, the best detective in town, was called
upon to solve the case of the missing cookies. Pre-schoolers can complete
a series of fun activities to help Kopi solve the mystery and learn important
lessons on honesty and integrity along the way.</p>
