<p>On 10 April 2026, three individuals were charged in Court with cheating
offences:</p>
<p>a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Lim Chee How, Nicholas</strong>  <strong>(“Lim”)</strong> (林志豪,
45-year-old male Singaporean), a Solutions Architect of ATOS Information
Technology (Singapore) Pte Ltd (“ATOS”) at the material time of the alleged
offence;</p>
<p>b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Chew Lian Wah (“Chew”)</strong> (邹联华,
51-year-old male Singaporean), a Director of SwiftX Solutions Pte Ltd (“SwiftX”)
at the material time of the alleged offence; and</p>
<p>c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Foo Jong Wan (“Foo”)</strong> (符永焕, 44-year-old
male Singaporean), a Director of SwiftX at the material time of the alleged
offence.</p>
<p>2. The charges allege that, in or around 2015, Lim engaged in a conspiracy
with Chew and Foo to cheat ATOS by dishonestly concealing the fact that
Lim had an interest in SwiftX being awarded the subcontract of a main contract
ATOS had with the Government. In pursuance of the conspiracy, Lim recommended
SwiftX to ATOS to be awarded the subcontract, thereby dishonestly inducing
ATOS to award the subcontract and pay $535,000 to SwiftX.</p>
<p>3. For the above, Lim, Chew and Foo each face a charge under Section 420
read with Section 109 of the <em>Penal Code</em>. In addition, Lim was charged
on 9 March 2026 with offences under the <em>Prevention of Corruption Act</em> for
allegedly corruptly receiving bribes in relation to the same main contract
ATOS had with the Government.</p>
<p>4. A person convicted of an offence under Section 420 of the <em>Penal Code</em> shall
be sentenced to imprisonment for a term of up to 10 years’ imprisonment,
and also be liable to fine.</p>
